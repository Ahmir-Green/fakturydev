VISION

FAKTÜRY represents a streetwear brand that releases exclusive products through limited quantity drops. Each product purchased will have an NFT (non-fungible token) that reflects the purchase. These NFT's are designed to eventually be used as wearables in the Metaverse on avatars. The item(s) purchased will be unique to the original minting # given at launch, will give access to special privileges, as well as apply a rarity factor. The native token $FAKTURY, will be used by holders for payments, staking, and access to members-only events and promotions.
